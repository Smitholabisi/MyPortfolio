The Analysis of 2017- 2020 NYC Taxi Trips dataset, focused on 28 millions Green Taxi Trips in New York City that provide street-hail and prearranged taxi services.

Summary of the Insights:
- NYC green taxi recorded 28m total trips, generating $373m total fare and facilitating the movement of $37m passangers across 5 service zones.
- The average fare was $13.18.
- The avarage distance durations was 3.87 minutes.
- The total of $5m was paid as toll fees.
- January was the highest record for the number of trips, with saturday fridays being the busiest days.
- The year will the highest trips was 2017 at $12m and the least year was 2020 at $2m.
- The credit card emerged as the preferred payment method for these trips at 57.24%. Street-hail has the highest percentage at 97.55%.
